Intro

Hello, I'm Erlisja Kore. I am currently a master's student in Information Science and Technology (IS&T) at Temple University. My passion for coding and programming began in high school when I learned HTML to build a simple webpage. However, I initially pursued a different path, earning a bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics from Albania due to my love for science and physics. In 2022, I moved to the US with my family and decided to switch careers to follow my passion for coding. This led me to enroll at Temple University to pursue a master's degree in IS&T.

I have solid experience in coding, particularly in Python and Java, where I am proficient. During my academic journey, I have taken classes on machine learning and database design and management, gaining valuable experience in SQL and MySQL. Additionally, I am proficient in Kotlin and have a passion for designing Android apps. I have participated in various hackathons, with one of my favorite projects being an Android app I built featuring a mental health chatbot. While my main expertise lies in backend development, I have recently started exploring front-end technologies to broaden my skill set. Projects

Random Recipe Generator App

The Random Recipe Generator is an Android app built using Kotlin. The app features a random recipe generator that instantly provides a random recipe from a diverse database of dishes using a recipe API. Additionally, it includes a recipe search bar for finding specific recipes by keywords, a "Save" button to easily access your favorite recipes later, and the ability to filter recipes by category such as breakfast, lunch, dinner, desserts, and more.

Technologies Used

Kotlin: Developed using Kotlin for a robust and efficient performance. Recipe API: Integrated with a random recipe generator API to provide various recipe options.
Android Studio: Built and tested in Android Studio for a seamless user experience.

About Me

Hi, I'm Erlisja!
I am a Software Engineer and Full Stack Developer with a passion for science and technology. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Information Science and Technology and I am also completing a professional training program as a Full Stack Engineer at Per Scholas.
My love for science and physics started with a simple desire to understand the world around me, which led me to study physics. At the same time, I was drawn to technology and the excitement of building and creating solutions, which led me to pursue software engineering. This path has allowed me to blend my passion for science with my interest in technology, giving me the tools to create solutions that make a real difference in people's lives. It's incredibly fulfilling to turn ideas into software that can help others and push the world forward.
I’m a quick learner who’s always eager to take on new challenges and enhance my skills. I continuously seek opportunities to grow and develop, pushing myself to become a better version of who I am.
